01 — Philosophy

The domain is
the pitch.

"Want this done for you? Forgewith.ai." The name is a sentence. The brand is an action. It tells you what happens before you even land on the site.

A verb, not a noun

"Forge with AI" is an action. It's not a company name you have to explain — it's an invitation. Every time someone says the domain, they're describing the product.

The Hormozi principle

Alex Hormozi is the brand. Acquisition.com is the vessel. Adrián Treviño is the brand. Forgewith.ai is the vessel. Followers associate the person with the domain — instantly.

Light, not hidden

Forge doesn't live in the shadows. Clean backgrounds, open space, nothing to hide. The orange demands attention. Confidence doesn't need a dark room.

Built to be said

"Go to forgewith.ai" — five syllables. Works in a Reel, a DM, a podcast, a cold email. No spelling it out. No "that's forge, F-O-R-G-E..." It just flows.

05 — Voice & Tone

Direct. Technical.
Never corporate.

Forge talks like a builder talks to another builder. The domain rolls off the tongue the same way.

✓ Do this
"Your homepage has zero lead capture. Every visitor is a missed lead. I can show you — forgewith.ai"
✕ Not this

✓ Do this
