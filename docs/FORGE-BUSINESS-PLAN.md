## 3. TARGET MARKET

### Who we sell to
US businesses doing $500K-$5M in annual revenue. Service-based and e-commerce. Owner-operated with reachable decision makers.

### Why this market
- **Highest willingness to pay** for marketing services globally
- **Broken funnels** — they have revenue but grew through word-of-mouth or one channel. Their digital presence is usually an afterthought.
- **Reachable decision makers** — the owner runs the Instagram, answers the email, accepts LinkedIn connections. No gatekeepers.
- **Clear ROI framing** — doing $1M, Forge helps add $200K, that's 40x return on $5K/mo. Easy math.
- **English speaking** — content, outreach, delivery all in one language.

### Initial verticals to target (start horizontal, niche after 3-5 clients)
| Vertical | Why it's good for Forge | Where to find them |
|----------|------------------------|-------------------|
| Med spas / dental / chiro | High LTV per patient, terrible funnels, owner-operated | Instagram, Google Maps |
| E-commerce ($1-3M Shopify) | Visible funnel gaps, data-driven, understand CAC | LinkedIn, Shopify communities |
| Coaches / consultants ($500K-$2M) | Already create content, need funnel infrastructure | Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn |
| Real estate teams | High commission = high budget, competitive market | LinkedIn, local business groups |
| Home services (roofing, HVAC, plumbing) | Growing businesses, rarely have digital sophistication | Google Maps, LinkedIn |

**Do NOT pick a niche from the couch.** Run the scanner on businesses across all five. After 3-5 clients, the market tells you where you close easiest, deliver best, and get the best results. THAT becomes the niche.

### Ideal client profile
- Revenue: $500K-$5M/year
- Has product-market fit (people buy what they sell — they just need more of them)
- Creates or is willing to create content
- Owner makes the buying decision
- Sees marketing as an investment, not an expense

### Disqualifying signals
- Revenue under $300K (can't sustain $5K/mo)
- "I need to think about it" after seeing scan results and the offer
- Wants to micromanage every deliverable
- No willingness to create content
- Haggling on founding price

## 5. CONTENT STRATEGY

### The 100 followers = 1 client formula

**The math:**
- 1,000 followers → 10 clients → $50-100K/month
- Requires: 1% follower-to-client conversion rate
- Achievable in B2B high-ticket IF content is targeted, funnel is airtight, and every follower sees enough proof to trust the capability

**The first 1,000 followers are the most valuable.** Early adopters who bet on Adrián before social proof exists. Most loyal, most engaged, most likely to convert or refer.

### Platform priority

| Platform | Role | Frequency | Content type |
|----------|------|-----------|-------------|
| Instagram | Primary discovery + trust | 5-7x/week | Reels (60-90s), carousels, stories daily |
| TikTok | Amplification (repurpose) | 3-5x/week | Reposted Reels + native edits |
| LinkedIn | B2B credibility + outreach | 3x/week | Text posts, case studies, thought leadership |
| YouTube | Long-form authority (Phase 2) | 1x/week when capacity allows | Deep dives, case studies, walkthroughs |

### Content pillars

**Pillar 1: "The build" (40%)**
Building Forge in public. Simultaneously content AND product demonstration.

Examples:
- "Watch me scan this business's funnel in 60 seconds and find $200K in missed revenue"
- "I built an AI that follows up with leads across email, SMS, and WhatsApp — here's how it works"
- "This homepage has 4 conversion killers. Here's what I'd change."
- Tool demos, before/after client work, behind-the-scenes of the tech

Every "build in public" post is a product demo disguised as content.

**Pillar 2: "The playbook" (35%)**
Actionable marketing, sales, and funnel advice. Pure value.

Examples:
- "3 things killing your landing page conversions right now"
- "The exact email sequence that books 30% more calls"
- "Your Instagram bio is losing you leads — here's the formula"
- "The $100M value equation explained in 60 seconds"

Positions Adrián as the expert. Every piece of advice they try and see results from builds trust. And the more they DIY, the more they realize they need someone to do it for them.

**Pillar 3: "The proof" (15%)**
Results, case studies, before/after.

Pre-clients:
- "I scanned 50 businesses this week. Here are the 5 most common funnel mistakes."
- "My quesabirrias business did $80K MXN/month. Here's the funnel that made it work."

With clients:
- "Client went from $40K/mo to $72K/mo in 60 days — here's every step"
- "3 months in: [Client]'s numbers before and after Forge"

**Pillar 4: "The person" (10%)**
Adrián's story, values, lifestyle. Not hustle porn — real, human.

Examples:
- "I paid for college selling quesabirrias at 5 AM. Now I build AI sales systems."
- "Why I'm moving to Dubai (and why it's not about the flex)"
- Day-in-the-life content

### Weekly production workflow

```
SUNDAY (1 hr): Plan the week
  - Review performance → pick topics → write 7-10 hooks/scripts (AI-assisted)

MON + WED (2 hrs each): Record batch
  - 5-7 talking head pieces per session
  - 2-3 screen recordings (tool demos, scan walkthroughs)
  - Raw files → editor

TUE + THU: Editor delivers → Adrián reviews → scheduling queue

DAILY: 3-5 stories + 15-20 min engagement (comments, DMs, prospect engagement)

FRIDAY: Analysis + outreach batch
  - Review content performance
  - Run 10-15 scans on targeted businesses
  - Batch outreach with scan results
```
